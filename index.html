What is MIStics?

Combining methodologies from mathematics, informatics and statistics to turn data into knowledge is the main focus in data science. MIStics is here to help to solve your data science related problems by providing end-to-end solutions and/or consulting.

Mathematics

Optimization, machine learning

Informatics

Algorithms, databases, modelling, etc, R, Python, Shiny, Flask, APIs etc, visualization

Statistics

Probabilities, models
